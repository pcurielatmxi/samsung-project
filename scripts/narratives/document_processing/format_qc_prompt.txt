## VERIFICATION TASK

Compare the formatted output against the input extraction. Check for:

1. **Document Metadata Accuracy**:

   - Are dates correctly formatted as YYYY-MM-DD?
   - Is the summary preserved from the extraction?
   - Is the author/originating party correct?

2. **Statement Completeness**:
   - Are all statements from the extraction represented in the output?
   - Is statement text preserved without truncation?
   - Are no statements missing or combined inappropriately?

3. **Category Accuracy**:
   - Are statement categories appropriate for the content?
   - delay: actual schedule delays
   - scope_change: added work or modifications
   - quality_issue: defects, rework
   - owner_direction: directions from owner
   - progress: status updates
   - design_issue: drawing/RFI issues
   - coordination: interface problems
   - dispute: contested items

4. **Data Preservation**:
   - Are party names preserved exactly?
   - Are locations captured (FAB, SUE, SUW, FIZ, grid lines, levels)?
   - Are quantified impacts (days, costs) correctly transcribed?
   - Are document references captured?

5. **No Hallucinations**:
   - All data in the output should come from the extraction
   - No invented dates, parties, or impacts
