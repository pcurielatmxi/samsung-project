## Overview

W.G. Yates & Sons Construction (Yates) is the General Contractor for the Samsung Taylor FAB1 project. All labor tracked in ProjectSight is Yates labor - either Yates self-perform or Yates subcontractors.

**Identification Rule:** All companies in ProjectSight labor data (`processed/projectsight/labor_entries.csv`) are confirmed Yates subs.
